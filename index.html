Welcome!

I am a web developer, using various languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My aim is to work in the tech industry to design beautiful, interactive, robust, and responsive websites. My roots are here in Louisville, where I was born and raised. I studied at the University of Louisville for my undergraduate studies and pursued a degree in Philosophy. Now I am seeking a career in coding as an avenue to express my creativity and help business and individuals achieve their dreams.

Work Background

Warehousing has been my career for the past ten years. I have worked in several roles, from team leadership to problem-solving, analyzing production metrics, shipping and receiving, forklift driving, and order fulfillment. I worked for Amazon for three years and I have worked for Northern Safety for the past six-and-a-half years. Before that I've worked in various roles of customer service and retail.

Hobbies

Watching TV shows is one of my favorite things to do. I love good story, comedy, drama, and shows that make you think. Some of my favorite shows are Breaking Bad, Stranger Things, the IT Crowd, and Avatar: The Last Airbender. Boardgaming is also near and dear to my heart. There are so many great boardgames out today, from simple card games to elaborate, complex strategy games. I think there's a boardgame for everyone! I also like going for walks and spending quality time with friends.
